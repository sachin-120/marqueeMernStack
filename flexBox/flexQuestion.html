One Day International (ODI) cricket is one of the most popular formats of the game, played between two international teams.making the format a balance between the patience of Test cricket and the fast-paced excitement of T20 cricket. Introduced in the early 1970s, ODI cricket has produced some of the sport's most memorable matches and legendary performances.

Test cricket is the oldest and most traditional format of the game, widely regarded as the ultimate test of a player's skill, technique, patience, and mental strength. A Test match is played over five days, with each team having the opportunity to bat and bowl twice. Unlike limited-overs cricket, there is no restriction on the number of overs, allowing teams to build innings carefully and develop long-term strategies.
